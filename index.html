About Us

At COCONUTCH OIL, we believe in the power of nature. Our brand name comes from Coconut, Nurture, and Touch, reflecting our dedication to creating pure, plant-based coconut oil that nurtures your skin, hair, and overall well-being.

Unlike mainstream products filled with synthetic additives, our coconut oils are 100% natural, locally sourced, and designed for those seeking an eco-conscious self-care alternative.

With plant-based infusions like lemongrass, peppermint, and origanum, our products enhance moisture retention, strengthen hair, and provide a soothing massage experience.

How to Order

Ordering from COCONUTCH OIL is quick and easy!

  1. Browse our products – Visit our online store and select your favorite coconut oil variant.
  2. Add to Cart – Click on your chosen product and add it to your cart.
  3. Checkout – View your cart, review the items, and proceed to checkout.
  4. Payment – Choose your preferred payment method (GCash or Cash on Delivery).
  5. Order Confirmation – Receive an email with your order details and tracking number.
  6. Delivery – Expect your order within 3-7 business days (depending on location).
